Data is most useful when it changes a decision. I'm a data analyst who works the whole arc — analyzing, interpreting, and visualizing data, then turning it into a story someone can act on. My foundation is in statistics, with working depth in business, machine learning, and deep learning, and I'm most fluent in SQL and Python.

I'm currently a Data Analyst on the DDO Innovation team at Krungsri, within the Data Science and AI department — building customer segmentation, running funnel analysis on app behavior, and supporting Voice and Agentic AI for QC operations. Before that I spent about a year and a half at Shopee across payment-solutions BI and seller experience, with earlier roles as a data scientist and analyst at ttb bank, Michelin, and foodpanda.

I studied Statistics at Chulalongkorn University, where my senior project benchmarked deep-learning models for Thai food image classification. I write about AI engineering on Medium — explaining something clearly is the best test of whether you actually understand it.
