I'm a Data Analyst on the DDO Innovation team at Krungsri, within the Data Science and AI department, where I build customer segmentation for renewal campaigns and dig through GO App funnels to find where users drop off. I started in statistics at Chulalongkorn, and I'm most fluent in SQL and BigQuery. Lately I've been working on Voice and Agentic AI for QC operations, which is also what I write about.

Before Krungsri I spent about a year and a half at Shopee across payment-solutions BI and seller experience, with earlier roles as a data scientist and analyst at ttb bank, Michelin, and foodpanda. The through-line is an analytics foundation I keep extending toward machine learning and, more recently, agentic systems.

My senior project at Chulalongkorn benchmarked deep-learning models for Thai food image classification. I write about AI engineering on Medium, lately about loop engineering and the design layers that sit above prompting.
